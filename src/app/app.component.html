My Planet is a evolutionsimulation which has been developed to explore the posibilities of neural networks.
It is heavely inspired by evo net by brotcrunsher The simulation aims to create a stable population of creatures by imitating evolution throw genetic algorithm.
The creatures forming the population have each a brain which is represented by a neural network which decides whether the creature should eat, move or give birth. Firstly the brain is configured randomly, but a child's brain is similar to it's parent's brain with a few mutations. In this way the children's brain may be better than the parent's, or so to say the child might be fitter than its parent. Um eine stabile Population zu erzeugen geht, braucht man nur die Simulation zu starten und einige Zeit (ca. 10-15 Mnuten) warten. Man kann auch vordefinierte fitte Geschöpfe hinzuzufügen mit Klick auf „Load Creatures...“, dann im Popup „very good creature“ (o.ä.) mit Klick auf „add“ hinzufügen. Es empfiehlt sich, mehrere Geschöpfe hinzuzufügen um sicherzustellen, dass aus den neuen Geschöpfen eine Population entsteht.
